Bike ride to Nomad Riverside Lodge for 2 days - 1 night

You have never had such a good chance to explore the deepest area of ​​Sapa valley than now. The roads are a combination of asphalt, gravel paths and dirt roads, and the trip is also a combination of cycling and cultural exploration. You will enjoy a vacation to remote and withering areas, far from mass tourism and overnight at the newly built Nomad Riverside Lodge homestay next to the edge of the forest and the beautiful stream. Your trip starts from Hanoi to Sapa by the luxurious Nomad Mountain Express, which is an 8-seat limousine with a modern, spacious and smooth interior design. Thanks to the new road connecting the highway to the mountains, you will reach Thanh Phu Bridge after only 4.5 hours on the bus, from here you will cycle to Nomad Riverside Lodge.

Schedule


Tour code: NS02

Activities: Moutain biking

Tỉme: 2 days 1 night - Can stay for 1 or more nights.


Day 1: Go to the mountains and cycle to Nomad Riverside Lodge


From 6:30 - 7:00 am, Nomad Mountain Express will pick you up from your hotel in Hanoi before departing for nearly 4.5 hours to Thanh Phu Bridge. From here you will travel a little further to the Tay village in Thanh Phu, where you will have lunch with the local host. In the afternoon the car will go a little higher. Now you are in the remote mountains of Sapa. This area is the main residence of Red Dao people. The beautiful views of the valley, waterfalls and terraced fields are all in sight. In the middle of the afternoon, you'll arrive at Nomad Riverside Lodge located right by the stream. This is where you relax your legs, swim or walk the village. The next meal is served right here. After the meal, you can take the time to try the traditional Red Dao bath experience, which is collected and prepared by the Red Dao women themselves.

Additional activity:

Accompany a native woman to choose her own herbal blend and spend the evening enjoying herself in the bath. Herbal bath and tour must be booked and paid in advance.


Day 2: Visit the Green H’mong village and explore the valley on bicycles


You will wake up in the fresh air of the village. Breakfast is served on the homestay's patio, just meters from the stream. Today you will explore other remote areas of Sapa. The bike ride requires a lot of concentration because it takes place mainly on tortuous roads. First, you will ride a bike to Nam Than village, the only inhabited place of the Green H’mong community in Sapa. Going deeper into the valley, you will soon come across the Red Dao village in Sin Chai. If it's school day, you'll be waved by babies with big round eyes. You will enjoy lunch at a stop on the road. In the early afternoon, you will return to Thanh Phu Bridge, board the Nomad Mountain Express and transfer to the hotel in Hanoi.

Caution:

- Many tourists regret not staying for one more night after a long distance from Hanoi.
- Nomad Mountain Express can only pick up and drop off passengers in Hanoi Old Quarter and Tay Ho District.
- Transportation and excursion trips are based on group coupling.
