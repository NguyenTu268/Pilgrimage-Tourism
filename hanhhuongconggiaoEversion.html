Pilgrimage to Our Lady of Ta Pao


Statue of Our Lady on the Ta Pao mountain (referred to as the statue of Our Lady of Ta Pao; French: Notre Dame de Ta Pao) is located in Dong Kho commune, Tanh Linh district, Binh Thuan province. This statue of Our Lady is cast in white cement 3m high, placed on a 2m high square pedestal. The complex of monumental works, the ceremony of Our Lady of Ta Pao is now one of the major pilgrimage centers of Vietnamese Catholics, officially known as the Mother Mother Center of Ta Pao.




Pilgrimage to Our Lady of Ta Pao



The legend: 1950 on the occasion of the National Congregation of the Holy Motherhood to commemorate the 100 Marian apparitions at Lourdes. President Ngo Dinh Diem instructed the Government General Palace of Dien Dien to build 5 monuments of Our Lady in the years 59, 60 and 61 in Giang Son (Dak Lak) - Thac Mo (Phuoc Long) - Phuong Hoang (Kontum) - Trinh Phong (Ninh Thuận) and Ta Pao (Bình Tuy now Binh Thuận). On 12 December 1959, the dedication of the Statue of Our Lady of Ta Pao was celebrated by the Reverend Marcello Piquet with the full attendance of priests, Religious and thousands of parishioners.



Pilgrimage group of Our Lady of Ta Pao



From 1964 to 1975, because of the laity war everywhere, the Statue of Our Lady was broken and forgotten.
