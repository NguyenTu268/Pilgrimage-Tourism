Day tour to Nam Cang village - the most remote area of ​​Sapa

Get to the mysterious Nam Cang area and walk along the biggest and most beautiful rice fields in Sapa, watch the waterfalls and enjoy a meal at a local homestay next to the edge of the forest and streams. This is the perfect opportunity to explore the natural life of ethnic minorities in the mountains. The natives here always welcome visitors to this place
