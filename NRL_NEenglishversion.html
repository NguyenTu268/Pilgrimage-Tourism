Resort package at Nomad Riverside Lodge & Nomad Ecolodge 3 days 2 nights

This is the perfect choice to explore the mysterious region of Sapa. Visit colorful ethnic communities, experience a cultural tour, walk through villages and spend the night in a Red Dao village and then in bungalows at Topas Ecolodge . Both resorts are located in beautiful locations outside the town of Sapa, deep in the peaceful mountains.

Schedule


Tour code: NS01

Activities: Moutain resorts

Time: 3 days 2 nights - Can stay for 1 or more nights


Day 1: From the Red River to Nomad Riverside Lodge in the remote mountains


From 6:30 to 7:00 am, Nomad Mountain Express will pick you up from your hotel in Hanoi and travel for nearly 5 hours to Thanh Phu Bridge, then go a short distance to Nam Cang village. Once you arrive, you will walk across the village, cross a small bridge and check in at Nomad Riverside Lodge. Your lunch is served at this homestay itself. The afternoon is an ideal time to visit the Red Dao village. Nam Cang is located in the most remote area of ​​Sa Pa and the Red Dao women always wear the most colorful and diverse clothes among Vietnam's ethnic minority communities. The Red Dao are very good at preparing herbal bath water, planting medicinal plants and embroidery. The guide will accompany you, explore the village and learn how the villagers make writing paper from rice, manipulate silver and embroidery. Back at your homestay, you might want to try a herbal bath. The ingredients for the bath water are made from various herbs, which are collected by the Red Dao women in the forest next to the homestay. You will also enjoy a cozy dinner here.

Additional activity:

Accompany a native woman to choose her own herbal blend and spend the evening enjoying herself in the bath. Herbal bath and tour must be booked and paid in advance.


Day 2: Walk along the stream, visit the forest. Move to Nomad Ecolodge


In the morning, you will walk into the forest to explore the beautiful natural scenery. You will meet locals walking on bare feet. The higher you go, the more you will be attracted by the natural scenery. Along the trail, you will come across a variety of plants, bamboo and herbs. Cardamom is grown in this area, which has a spicy flavor and is used to cook traditional dishes. Listening to the sounds of the mountains and birds, you will find a clear stream flowing from the mountains. This is an interesting stop for you to enjoy nature, soak your feet in the cool water. You will spend some time to explore the surrounding forest before returning. Leaving the mountain, you return to the village at noon. You may want to swim in the stream if weather permits before enjoying lunch. At 14:50, the bus takes you to Nomad Ecolodge. From the balcony of your room, you can enjoy the beautiful view of the mountains you have just passed.

Day 3: Back to Hanoi


The morning is your relaxing time at Nomad Ecolodge. You can take a break or borrow a high quality mountain bike from the resort to explore the surrounding area. You can also schedule a bus to Sapa to visit the town or book a half-day tour to explore the roof of Indochina, Fansipan peak (3,143 m) by cable car. Lastly, you may want to book a massage session at our newly opened spa. At 15:00, the Nomad Moutain Express will depart from Nomad Ecolodge and return to your hotel in Hanoi.

Caution:

- Nomad Mountain Express can only pick up and drop off passengers in Hanoi Old Quarter and Tay Ho District.
- Transportation and excursion trips are based on group coupling.
